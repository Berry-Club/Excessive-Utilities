**Drops of Evil** are rare drops from Wither Skeletons that can perform a couple functions.

When used on dirt, it will convert a wide area of it into [Cursed Earth](@excessive_utilities:cursed_earth).

When used on a Mob Spawner, it will convert it into a [Resturbed Mob Spawner].
