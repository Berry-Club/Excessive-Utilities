The **Magical Boomerang** is a throwable item that can be used to attack enemies and pick up items at a distance.

When thrown, the Boomerang will fly in a straight line until it hits an entity or block, or after a certain distance. It will then return to the player, moving through any blocks in the way.

If the Boomerang hits an entity, it will deal 4 damage (2 hearts) of damage.

## Enchantments

- [Bladerang](@excessive_utilities:bladerang)
- [Boomereaperang](@excessive_utilities:boomereaperang)
- [Burnerang](@excessive_utilities:burnerang)
- [Kaboomerang](@excessive_utilities:kaboomerang)
- [Zoomerang](@excessive_utilities:zoomerang)

## Configuration

The radius that the Boomerang will pick up dropped items, as well as the radius that the Boomereaperang enchantment will break Grass, can both be configured.
