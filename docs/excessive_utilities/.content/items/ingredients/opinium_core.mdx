The **Opinium Core** is a multi-stage crafting ingredient.

It comes in nine tiers:

- Pathetic
- Mediocre
- Passable
- Decent
- Good
- Damn Good
- Amazing
- Inspiring
- Perfected

Each tier requires 2 of the previous tier, as well as something extra. That means a single Perfected Opinium Core requires:

- 1024 Red Coal
- 4609 Iron Ingots
- 2304 Gold Ingots
- 1152 Diamonds
- 576 Emeralds
- 32 Chorus Fruits
- 16 Experience Bottles
- 8 Elytras
- 4 Nether Stars
