The **Healing Axe** is comparable to a Diamond Axe, but has infinite durability.

More importantly, however, holding it in your hand will occasionally feed you a bit.

Additionally, attacking an entity with the Healing Axe will **transfer some of your health to it**. For undead mobs, they will instead be damaged for 4 times that amount.

## Configuration

- The chance per tick to feed while held, defaulting to 1/100.
- The amount of food and saturation, defaulting to 1 food and 0.2 saturation.
- The amount of health transferred on hit, defaulting to 4 health (2 hearts).
