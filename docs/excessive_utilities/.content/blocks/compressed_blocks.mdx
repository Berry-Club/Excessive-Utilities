**Compressed Blocks** store large amounts of common terrain blocks in a single block space.

The available families are:

- Compressed Cobblestone
- Compressed Cobbled Deepslate
- Compressed Dirt
- Compressed Gravel
- Compressed Sand

Each family has 16 compression tiers. Higher tiers are denser and have higher hardness and blast resistance, but the blocks are otherwise storage variants of their base material.
