**Trash Cans** instantly delete any item, fluid, or energy put into them (depending on which is used).

The Item and Fluid Trash Cans have can have an [Item Filter](@excessive_utilities:item_filter) or [Fluid Filter](@excessive_utilities:fluid_filter) inserted, which will prevent what is allowed to be inserted.
