**Ender Lilies** are a special flower that can be found in the outer End islands.

They grow in 7 stages. At their maximum age, they can be interacted with to drop an Ender Pearl.

Ender Lilies, like Endermen, hate water. If they get wet, they'll teleport away. This includes the [Watering Can](@excessive_utilities:watering_can), but *not* the [Reinforced Watering Can](@excessive_utilities:reinforced_watering_can).
