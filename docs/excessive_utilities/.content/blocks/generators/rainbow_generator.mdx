The **Rainbow Generator** outputs an infinite amount of FE per tick, but **only if all other Generator types are active!**

Every player has a Generator Network. Any Generator that they place is part of it.

Every so often, the player's Generator Network will check their Generators. If they have at least one of each type of Generator running, their placed Rainbow Generator will start outputting FE.

Note that you can only have a single Rainbow Generator per player!
