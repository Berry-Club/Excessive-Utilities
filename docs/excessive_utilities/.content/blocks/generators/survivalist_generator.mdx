The **Survivalist Generator** burns furnace fuel to produce FE. Fuel will burn very low and very slow.

Furnace fuel will last for 100 times longer in the Survivalist Generator than it would in a Furnace, producing 5 FE/t.

## Configuration

Both the burn time and FE/t can be configured.
